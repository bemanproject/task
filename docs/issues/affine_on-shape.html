affine_on shape may be wrong

Section: 13.13.3 [exec.affine.on]

Submitter: Dietmar Kühl

Discussion:

affine_on is specified to take a sender and a scheduler as arguments. The scheduler is meant to match the scheduler obtained from the get_scheduler query on the receiver's environment. Thus, the scheduler argument is redundant and the semantics become weird if these two schedulers don't match.
